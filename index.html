Determining Durations for Active Monitoring — Ebola

A static reimplementation of the activemonitr Ebola outputs, verified against R. All statistics computed in your browser.

Disease parameters for the modelled pathogen (Ebola). The incubation period is fitted from outbreak data and validated against the R model. The remaining parameters are placeholders for the onward-transmission modelling step — shown to map out the interface; they are not yet used in any calculation.

The point shows the estimated median and 95th-percentile incubation period for Ebola; the shaded region is the KDE credible region over the posterior (median × 95th-percentile space).
